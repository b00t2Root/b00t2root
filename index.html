About Us

B00t2root is a virtual gathering of all the CyberSecurity enthusiasts to enrich their skills in the field of Information Security and would serve as a great platform to find better solutions to real life security threats solving the challenges of this competitive jeopardy style CTF.

What do I have to do?

You will be presented with challenges . The ultimate goal of every challenge is to find a "flag". A flag looks like this: b2r{this-is-a-flag}, and the method in which ypu find the flag depends on the challenge.
You can always contact us if you feel that a challenge is broken.

Is there a limit for team size?

Yes, there is size limit of 4 participants, lone wolves are also welcome :)

Do I have to stay online the whole time?

Of course not. Don't forget to eat and sleep.
